Important Notice

It gives us immense pleasure to announce that our Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi will be visiting our school on the special occasion of World Environment Day. This is a moment of great pride and honor for our entire school community. The visit is organized to highlight the importance of environmental conservation and to encourage young minds to take active steps towards building a cleaner, greener, and healthier India. During the event, our respected Prime Minister will interact with students, plant saplings on the school premises, and share his valuable thoughts on environmental protection and sustainability. All students, teachers, and staff members are requested to assemble in the school auditorium by 8:00 a.m. sharp, dressed neatly in proper school uniform. The event will include: A special welcome ceremony for the Hon’ble Prime Minister. Cultural performances focusing on nature and environmental themes. Poster and slogan displays prepared by students on the topic “Our Earth, Our Responsibility.” A pledge ceremony to protect and preserve our environment. Students are also encouraged to participate enthusiastically by creating eco-friendly decorations, awareness posters, and short speeches emphasizing the importance of saving our planet. Discipline, cleanliness, and cooperation from everyone are expected throughout the day. Let us all come together to make this event a grand success and demonstrate our dedication towards a greener future. Let the visit of our Hon’ble Prime Minister be a true inspiration for every student to take meaningful action for the betterment of our environment. Together, let’s make our school and our Earth shine with green pride!
